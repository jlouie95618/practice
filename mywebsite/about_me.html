Experience:

I'll keep this next portion short: I am currently a junior studying Computer Science at Stanford University. You can see my résumé here. I am fascinated by the intersection between medicine/healthcare and technology and strongly believe that it is one of the few great frontiers that technology has yet to truly revolutionize. Owing to my interest in medicine and healthcare, I have taken numerous classes in Human Biology in addition to my computer science classes. I am also very interested in education and am an active section leader for Stanford Univeristy's introductory computer sciences courses: CS106A, CS106B and CS106X.

In addition to school, during the summer of 2014, I conducted bioinformatics research in the Ashley Lab at the Stanford University School of Medicine, and during the summer of 2015, I was a software engineering intern for the start up Airtable.
